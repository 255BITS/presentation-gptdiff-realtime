# portrait

A photographic, eye-level portrait of Vera — a young woman with an open, intelligent face,
warm hazel eyes, dark hair loosely tied back with a few strands loose. Soft natural light
from the left; a faint cyan rim-light on the right edge, the glow of open screens nearby.
A calm half-smile, present and curious, looking just past the camera as if mid-conversation.
Plain dark background, shallow depth of field, 50mm, gentle film grain. No text, no logos.
